About Me

Hi, I’m Pooja Karuvan, I am a full-stack web developer who loves blending logic with imagination. I enjoy building full-stack web apps, creating magic-themed games, and solving real-world problems through technology using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB and Phaser.js.

I began my coding journey by completing comprehensive online courses, many of which were available on YouTube and working on hands-on projects. Since then, I have developed a variety of applications, ranging from e-commerce platforms to interactive in-browser games. I am passionate about bringing ideas to life through code and continuously seek to expand my knowledge of new technologies.

Whether I’m implementing JWT authentication or crafting a pixel-perfect spell-casting game, I bring a strong attention to detail and a passion for problem-solving. I’m currently deepening my backend skills and expanding my knowledge of full-stack development.

I'm open to exciting opportunities in web development, especially roles that let me build creative and engaging user experiences.
